Your Safety Our Concern

Any project comes ethics, security, legal and moral issues. We have learned that if we want to run an ecommerce business it is very important to learn ethical importance of our business. Ethics are not only good for the soul but also for the bottom line. The ecommerce traction is the biggest problem going to arise for us because customers give their lots of personal information like their address, credit card numbers, the email addresses, and passwords which most of the customers share across any accounts. This may lead us in losing our customers trust. The concern for the privacy has become major with the rise of identity theft as well as impersonation. We must take care that the exchanged information must not be tampered or altered be anyone. The third privacy issue arising will be authentication. The most important issue arises for us is to keep a proof of the exchanged information. The legal issues which we are thinking that we might face is Incorporation problem, Copyright protection, Transaction issues and Trade mart security problem. Trademark security is one of the most common legal issues and can be a threat to our start-up. Efficient delivery system as well as proper effective supply chain and service management is must. We need to ensure that the goods and services provided by the shopkeepers to our customer follow a fair-trade practise. It may be damaged, expired, or different from the ordered brand so our customers may be asking us to remove defects/deficiencies, replace the order or may be return the cost which they already paid. We must take care that they should compensate, or they should restrictively trade practice and take care next time. Moral issues related to harassment may arise. The delivery person or the shopkeeper should be avoided with direct attachment with the customer
